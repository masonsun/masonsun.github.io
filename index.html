whoami

Hi there! I'm Mason. I was born in Richmond, which is known for its rainy weather, Asian community, and parking etiquette (no causation, probably). My parents are from Taiwan, but I lived in Vancouver, Taipei, Istanbul, and Eastern Europe, each for four or more years, growing up. I guess home is more of a concept than a physical entity.

I received my bachelor's degrees in computer science and biology from McGill University. I'm currently a graduate student at Columbia University, pursuing a master's degree in data science.

I'm a data-driven individual with particular interests in machine learning and analytics. I also like delving into computational biology on the side. I maintain an inventory of things I enjoy reading, sharing, or learning that I call "Pursuing Knowledge." Please consider this knowledge in the loosest of definitions. Feel free to check it out.

echo exp

I was a research assistant at McGill University, where I studied the features of the human-virus interactome under the supervision of Brandon Xia. I have also been involved in other research efforts related to microbiology, bioengineering, and biomimetics at McGill, and cognitive science and functional genomics at the University of Hong Kong and Academia Sinica/National Taiwan University, respectively.

I was a machine learning intern at Umbo CV, an early stage startup that utilizes deep learning to deliver end-to-end surveillance solutions. I have also been a data science intern on the Data and Analytics Team at KPMG.
